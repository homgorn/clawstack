# Cost Rules

## Priority order

1. Free models for routine work when quality is acceptable.
2. Cheap default model for most day-to-day tasks.
3. Better research model for higher-value discovery work.
4. Premium model only for hard cases or tool-heavy chains.

## Guardrails

- Do not use premium models for greetings or simple drafting.
- Keep heartbeat on a cheap model.
- Review daily and weekly spend.
